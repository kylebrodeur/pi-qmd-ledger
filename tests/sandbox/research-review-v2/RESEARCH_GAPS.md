# Research Gaps & Information Needs
This document tracks "Information Gaps" identified during the research review process. 

## Protocol: The "No-Info" Trigger
During the review of `nlm` (NotebookLM) query responses:
1. If a response contains phrases like *"No information found,"* *"The sources do not mention,"* or *"Insufficient data to answer,"* this is a **Critical Research Gap**.
2. These gaps must be recorded here with:
    - The original query.
    - The specific missing piece of information.
    - A proposed research direction (new keywords, new sources, or specific papers).
3. This list serves as the backlog for the next "Sourcing" phase to expand the notebooks.
