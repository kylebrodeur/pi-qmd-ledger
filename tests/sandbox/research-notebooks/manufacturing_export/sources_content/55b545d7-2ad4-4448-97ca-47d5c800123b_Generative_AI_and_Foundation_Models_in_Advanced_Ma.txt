Introduction

The rise of Generative AI is revolutionising industries, and this masterclass offers a deep dive into how these cutting-edge technologies can transform the Manufacturing sector. Tailored for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) and industry leaders, this workshop moves beyond generic applications like ChatGPT, focusing on real-world implementations of Generative AI for factory-specific solutions.

Designed to address the unique needs of the local Manufacturing industry, the masterclass covers critical areas such as Quality Assurance, Predictive Maintenance, and Industrial Automation. Participants will learn from domain experts about practical use cases, current limitations, and the transformative potential of Gen-AI in Manufacturing.

The hands-on sessions will demonstrate how to harness open-source Gen-AI tools, create custom AI models using proprietary data, and explore the future of AI-driven manufacturing.

Through demonstrations, tutorials, and group workshops, attendees will gain practical insights and collaborative experience, equipping them to drive innovation and enhance productivity within their organisations.

Why This MasterClass

Tailored AI Solutions for Unique Needs

Customisation is Key:

 Understand the critical importance of adapting generative AI models to meet the specific needs and data environments of small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). Learn how to create solutions that integrate seamlessly with your existing systems, ensuring alignment with your unique scale, resources, and operational requirements.

Bridging the Gap Between Research and Industry

From Theory to Practise:

 Explore how cutting-edge academic research can be translated into practical, scalable solutions for manufacturing challenges. Gain insights on moving generative AI from proof-of-concept to deployment, addressing real-world issues like defect detection, predictive maintenance, and production line optimisation.

Hands-On Learning Experience

Practical Applications:

 Engage in interactive workshops that guide you through the customisation process, providing hands-on experience in developing AI models tailored for your business needs.

Industry-Specific Insights

Targeted Solutions for Manufacturing:

 Delve into the unique challenges faced by SMEs in the manufacturing sector and discover how generative AI can provide targeted solutions that enhance efficiency and reduce costs.

Networking with Experts and Peers

Build Connections:

 Connect with industry leaders, researchers, and fellow SMEs, fostering collaborations that can lead to innovative AI implementations and shared learning.

Future-Proofing Your Business

Stay Competitive:

 Equip your organisation with the knowledge to leverage AI effectively, ensuring you remain competitive in an increasingly digital landscape.

By attending this masterclass, you'll gain the skills and insights needed to harness the power of generative AI, driving meaningful improvements in your operations and setting your business up for long-term success. Don't miss this opportunity to customise AI solutions that work for you!

Who Should Attend

This masterclass is specifically designed for CEOs, CTOs, technical leaders, engineering managers, supervisors, product managers, system designers, design engineers, R&D engineers, application engineers, sales engineers, inspection and vision technicians, and new hires from various manufacturing sectors.

Dr Jamie Ng Suat Ling

Dr Jamie Ng Suat Ling

 is a Senior Principal Scientist and Head of the Advanced & Sustainable Manufacturing Division at A*STAR I²R. She leads projects in AI, analytics, automation, connectivity, and cybersecurity for manufacturing and aerospace, including predictive maintenance, IIoT for Industry 4.0, and robotics. As the Programme Lead for the Smart Automated Aircraft Visual Inspection System (SAAVIS), she focuses on automating defect detection in large structures like aircraft. Dr Ng holds a PhD in Human Factors Engineering, an MSc in Smart Product Design, and a BEng in Mechanical Engineering from Nanyang Technological University.. She has several patents and has received the Tan Kah Kee Young Inventors' Award and the IES Prestigious Engineering Award 2023.

Close

Dr Albertus Hendrawan Adiwahono

close

Dr Albertus Hendrawan Adiwahono

 is a Principal Scientist and Mobility Unit Leader in the Robotics and Autonomous Systems Division at A*STAR I²R. He leads the development of various robotics prototypes and technology software stacks referred to as I²R Navigation Stack (Navstack). I²R Navstack has been licenced to private companies and has been the core technology to various robotics platforms selected by Singapore government agencies for its robustness and cutting-edge capabilities for operation in actual dynamic environments. Dr Albertus holds a PhD in Mechanical Engineering from the National University of Singapore, with an M.Eng and B.Eng in Electrical Engineering from Bandung Institute of Technology, Indonesia. Notable robots and awards received include the MATAR security robot (International GovInsider Innovation Awards 2018 and MTI Firefly Silver Award 2021), SAAVIS aircraft inspection robot (IES Prestigious Engineering Awards 2023), and ASLAN humanoid robot (champion of RoboCup 2010: Robot Soccer World Cup).
