What is industrial AI?

Industrial AI is AI purpose-built for the real world: The factories, buildings, trains, power grids, and hospitals that keep our economies running. Unlike consumer AI, industrial AI must work in environments where reliability, safety, and precision are critical priorities.

People in the loop

While industrial AI excels at data processing and automation, humans provide the accountability and contextual judgment to navigate real-world nuances. These three stories show how experts use AI to drive safety, efficiency, and continuous improvement.

 

DAIRY MAINTENANCE MANAGER

A manager at Sachsenmilch used Senseye AI to find a pump failure weeks early. This let the team fix it during a planned stop, saving a six-figure sum and preventing the loss of thousands of liters of milk.

How AI supports manufacturing operations

https://www.siemens.com/en-us/company/insights/sachsenmilch-dairy-predictive-maintenance/

 

WATER UTILITY OPERATOR

Operators in Spain use SIWA Leak Finder to catch leaks as small as 0.2 liters per second. By pinpointing 10,000 events, they dispatch crews with precision, saving water and lowering costs for the community.

How AI manages building and data center systems

https://www.siemens.com/en-us/company/insights/ai-apps-protect-water-resources-spain/

 

WATER DEVELOPMENT ENGINEER

At VA SYD, engineers trained AI to monitor 2,000 km of pipe. It caught tiny leaks that sensors missed, cutting water loss from 10% to 8% and preventing major bursts before they could disrupt the city's supply.

How AI supports transport and logistics operations
