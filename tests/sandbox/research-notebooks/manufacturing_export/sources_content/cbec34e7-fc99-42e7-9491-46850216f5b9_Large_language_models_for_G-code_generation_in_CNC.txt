Large language models for G-code generation in CNC machining: A comparison of ChatGPT-3.5 and ChatGPT-4o 

Šket, K.a,*, Potočnik, D.a, Brezocnik, M.a, Ficko, M.a, Klančnik, S.a aUniversity of Maribor, Faculty of Mechanical Engineering, Maribor, Slovenia   A B S T R A C T  A R T I C L E   I N F O This research explores the viability of producing ISO G-code for 3-axis ma-chining with OpenAI's Chat Generative Pre-Trained Transformer models, particularly ChatGPT-3.5 and the newer GPT-4o. G-code (RS-274-D, ISO 6983) converts human directives into commands that machines can understand, controlling toolpaths, spindle velocities, and feed rates to produce particular aspects of an object. Previously, G-code was generated either by hand or through the use of computer-aided manufacturing (CAM) software along with machine-specific post-processors, both of which may require considerable time and expense. This research aimed to assess the practicality and effec-tiveness of specific large language models (LLMs) in generating G-code. The assessment took place in three distinct phases on a sample component that required 3-axis machining. These phases included: (1) the self-generated production of G-code for the sample component, (2) the examination of the independently generated G-code in the CAM application, and (3) the recogni-tion and justification of mistakes in the G-code. The outcomes indicated vary-ing abilities with promising findings. This method could accelerate and possi-bly enhance manufacturing workflows by decreasing reliance on expensive CAM software and specialized knowledge. 

 Keywords: 

Generative artificial intelligence; Intelligent manufacturing; Large language models (LLM); ChatGPT; CNC machining; G-code programming

Uporaba velikih jezikovnih modelov za generiranje G-kode pri CNC-obdelavi: primerjalna študija modelov ChatGPT-3.5 in ChatGPT-4o  

Šket, K.a,*, Potočnik, D.a, Brezocnik, M.a, Ficko, M.a, Klančnik, S.a 

aUniversity of Maribor, Faculty of Mechanical Engineering, Maribor, Slovenia 

  

P O V Z E T E K  P O D A T K I   O   Č L A N K U 

Ta raziskava preučuje izvedljivost generiranja ISO G-kode pri triosni obdelavi z modeli ChatGPT-3.5 in GPT-4o (angl. Chat Generative Pre-Trained Trans-former) podjetja OpenAI. G-koda (RS-274-D, ISO 6983) pretvarja človeška navodila v strojne ukaze, ki nadzorujejo poti orodja, hitrosti vretena in poda-jalne hitrosti za oblikovanje posameznih značilnosti obdelovanca. Doslej se je G-koda ustvarjala ročno ali z uporabo programske opreme za računalniško podprto izdelavo (CAM) skupaj s postprocesorji, prilagojenimi posameznim strojem, pri čemer oba pristopa zahtevata precej časa in stroškov. Namen te raziskave je bil oceniti praktičnost in učinkovitost izbranih velikih jezikovnih modelov (LLM) pri generiranju G-kode. Ocenjevanje je potekalo v treh ločenih fazah pri vzorčnem obdelovancu za triosno obdelavo: (1) samodejno generi-ranje G-kode za vzorčni obdelovanec, (2) preverjanje neodvisno ustvarjene G-kode v aplikaciji CAM in (3) prepoznavanje ter pojasnjevanje napak v G-kodi. Rezultati so pokazali različno stopnjo uspešnosti in obetavne ugotovitve. Takšen pristop bi lahko pospešil in izboljšal proizvodne procese z zmanjša-njem odvisnosti od dragih CAM-programov in zahtevnega specializiranega znanja.
